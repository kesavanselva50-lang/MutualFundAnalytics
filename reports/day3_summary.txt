# Day 3 Report – Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

1. NAV Trend Analysis

   * Analyzed daily NAV data for 40 mutual fund schemes from 2022–2025.
   * Visualized NAV movement using Plotly interactive charts.
   * Highlighted 2023 market bull run and 2024 correction phases.

2. AUM Growth Analysis

   * Compared Assets Under Management across major fund houses.
   * Created grouped bar charts for 2022–2025.
   * Identified SBI Mutual Fund as the market leader with the highest AUM.

3. SIP Inflow Trend Analysis

   * Examined monthly SIP inflows from January 2022 to December 2025.
   * Identified a continuous growth trend in retail participation.
   * Annotated the all-time high SIP inflow of ₹31,002 crore.

4. Category Inflow Heatmap

   * Visualized category-wise net inflows using a heatmap.
   * Compared inflows across Equity, Debt, Hybrid, Liquid, and Sectoral categories.
   * Identified categories attracting the highest investor interest.

5. Folio Count Growth Analysis

   * Tracked industry folio growth over the analysis period.
   * Observed growth from 13.26 crore folios to 26.12 crore folios.
   * Demonstrated significant expansion of retail investor participation.

6. NAV Return Correlation Analysis

   * Calculated daily returns for selected mutual fund schemes.
   * Generated correlation matrix heatmaps to study relationships among fund performances.

7. Sector Allocation Analysis

   * Aggregated portfolio holdings across equity funds.
   * Created sector allocation donut charts.
   * Identified major sector exposures including Banking, Financial Services, Utilities, Pharma, and Diversified sectors.

8. Investor Demographics Analysis

   * Generated age-group distribution charts.
   * Analyzed SIP amount distribution by age category.
   * Created gender participation visualizations.

9. Geographic Distribution Analysis

   * Evaluated investment activity by state.
   * Compared T30 and B30 city participation levels.
   * Visualized regional investment concentration.

Key Findings

* SIP inflows increased significantly during the analysis period, reaching a record ₹31,002 crore.
* Mutual fund folios nearly doubled from 13.26 crore to 26.12 crore.
* SBI Mutual Fund maintained leadership in total AUM.
* Liquid Funds and Flexi Cap Funds attracted substantial investor inflows.
* Banking and Financial sectors represented major portfolio allocations.
* Retail participation continued to strengthen across both T30 and B30 cities.
* Investor participation was concentrated within the 26–45 age group.
